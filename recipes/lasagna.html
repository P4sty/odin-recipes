Recipe for Lasagna

A tasty lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of wide pasta sheets, rich meat or vegetable sauce, creamy b√©chamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella, all baked together until golden and bubbly.

Ingredients:

  • Ground Beef
  • Garlic and onion
  • tomatoes in a can or fresh
  • Italian spices
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Mozzarella cheese

How to make your lasagna step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
